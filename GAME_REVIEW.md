## System overview — KPI-linked bonus distribution

### Design principles
- **Fair:** Monthly XP resets on the 1st — new joiners aren't penalised by veterans' history
- **Clear:** Two metrics only — roles posted (×100 XP) and shortlists (×50 XP)
- **Gamified:** Leaderboard sorted by monthly output, "% of team total" shown, bonus formula visible to all
- **Transparent:** Every recruiter sees exactly why they earned their share

### Bonus formula
```
Your bonus share (%) = Your monthly XP / Team total monthly XP × 100
Your payout = Your share % × Bonus Pool (set by manager each month)
```

### Monthly metrics (server-tracked, auditable)
| Action | XP | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Role posted this month | +100 | `_role.created_at >= month start` |
| Candidate shortlisted this month | +50 | `_shortlists.created_at >= month start` |

Deliberately excluded from monthly XP (to keep it clean and auditable):
- AI-generated matches: passive, not recruiter effort
- Achievement bonuses: one-time milestones, would inflate veteran scores
- Cumulative intros: `_matches` doesn't track `introduced_at` timestamp

## Data model

### Monthly XP (for bonus)
- Computed server-side in `GET /api/game/leaderboard`
- `monthlyRoles`: count of `_role` where `created_by = recruiterId AND created_at >= firstOfMonth`
- `monthlyShortlists`: count of `_shortlists` where `recruiter_id = recruiterId AND created_at >= firstOfMonth`
- `monthlyXP = monthlyRoles × 100 + monthlyShortlists × 50`
- Returned alongside `totalXP` (career), leaderboard sorted by `monthlyXP DESC, totalXP DESC`

### Career XP (for rank/level)
- Computed in `computeGameStats` (frontend)
- Uses all-time counts from `useRoles` + `shortlistCount` query
- Drives: level badge, rank title, level-up celebration, achievements
